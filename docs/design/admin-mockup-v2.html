Platform Overview
Industry Night is a platform for discovering, promoting, and managing industry events for creative workers — hair stylists, makeup artists, photographers, videographers, directors, and producers.

The Admin Console (this application) is for platform operators. The Social App (iOS/Android) is for attendees.
Events
Create, publish, and manage industry nights with Posh ticketing sync.
Community
Moderate feed posts, verify users, manage specialties.
Revenue
Manage sponsors, vendors, job posters, and data product exports.
